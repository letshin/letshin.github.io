Bioreactors and Fermenters for Chemical and Biological Processes:

Effective control and monitoring strategies are essential for precision fermentation in bioreactors, fermenters, and chemical reactors. Our company has created advanced sensing, monitoring, and control systems with remote data monitoring, recording, and access capabilities. We have achieved success and have collaborated with innovative companies and universities. Our sensing systems now include GHG MRV, enhancing our capabilities. We offer specific lighting, design, production, and control systems which can be seen in our website. This is particularly useful for algal bioreactors for underwater lighting.
