Aquaculture

Remote Aquaculture Monitoring and Responsive Rejuvenation:

The aquaculture industry, a rapidly expanding source of protein production, demands precise monitoring and control measures. With the risk of fish mortality within minutes of system failures, we have developed specialised sensing systems for both extensive pond-based and intensive RAS approaches. In extensive systems, our floating solution autonomously roams the pond, measuring, monitoring, and applying tailored control strategies. Through our collaboration with an innovative aquaculture company, we have achieved substantial yield improvements (up to 100%) depending on the fish species and growth methods. We monitor key parameters, including ammonium, nitrates, COD/BOD, dissolved oxygen, pH, and EC, ensuring optimal pond oxygenation with 3D monitoring.

ROAMER: Autonomous Pond Management:

ROAMER, our autonomous system is designed to map extensive pond or cage systems and maintain continuous oxygenation using smart valves. With the capacity to monitor essential parameters in the water, ROAMER consistently enhances fish quality while reducing the risk of colony collapse.
