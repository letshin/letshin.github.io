SMART - Specific Measurable Monitored Actuated Recorded Telemetric - Systems

We have designed our proprietary sensors to work in a SMART manner. SMART stands for Specific Measurable Monitored Actuated Recorded Telemetric- Agro-Theranostic Systems for Agriculture.

Smart Farms

Our sensors have been designed to work in open environments to convert conventional farms into smart farms.

They can be used in controlled environment systems.

Our systems measure over 30 parameters and provide comprehensive information whilst not requiring too many nodes within a given installation. This is due to our approach of 1-sensor - multiple output and inferring data instead of direct measurements.

This ensures that we are amongst the cheapest sensor based systems globally with cost savings of up to 500 times over the entire span of the farm!

Our system is able to record all of the data and provide it at your fingertips. Over time, our system applies advanced algorithms to learn from the ‘farmer’s expertise’ and apply it for future crop cycles and yields automatically.
