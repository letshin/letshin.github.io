Test Cells

Customisable Solutions for Your Needs:

Our test cells deliver cost-effective solutions while maintaining technical excellence. A key feature is their flexibility, with the ability to equip 24-42 stirrers (8-14 per level) for conducting multiple concurrent experiments. Easy component addition or replacement minimises downtime. You can expand your setup gradually to match your budget and research needs.

Terabithia sensors are modular and easily integrated within the Terabithia software ecosystem.

We have customisable solutions which come in the form of foldable mylar or premium stainless steel finish. Please contact us at sales@terabithia.co.uk to to discuss further and work on a customised quote.

Housing

Choose from two housing options to suit your needs. Our standard housing is a foldable mylar model

Compatability

Terabithia Test Cells are modular and can talk with other sensor systems under the Terabithia umbrella.

Lighting

Our lighting options include specific wavelengths for tailored growth; high-intensity lights; customizable intensity and frequency control; light sensors; the choice for two lights per shelf; and flexible auto ON/OFF settings.

Add-ons Possible

Explore our array of add-on features from AI cameras and carbon dioxide circulation to pH probes and readers. Elevate your research capabilities with our comprehensive solutions.
